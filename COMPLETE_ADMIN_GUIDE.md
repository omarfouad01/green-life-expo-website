## ğŸ¨ How to Change Colors

Colors use HSL format: `hue saturation% lightness%`

**Examples:**
- Green: `130 45% 30%`
- Blue: `210 80% 50%`
- Red: `0 70% 50%`

**HSL Values:**
- **Hue**: 0-360 (color on the wheel)
  - Red: 0
  - Green: 120
  - Blue: 240
- **Saturation**: 0-100% (color intensity)
- **Lightness**: 0-100% (brightness)
